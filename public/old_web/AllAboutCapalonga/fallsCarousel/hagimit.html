Hagimit Falls

Brgy. Villa Aurora Capalonga


The abundance of Hagimit trees within both side of the streams prompt the local villagers to name this striking postcard worthy waterfalls after these ancient medicinal plant.



Guest will need to walk 20-30 mins. from Brgy. Hall of Villa Aurora to this spot that is perfect for picnic excursions, swimming and diving.
