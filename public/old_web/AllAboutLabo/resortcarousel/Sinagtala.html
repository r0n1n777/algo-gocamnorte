SINAGTALA RESORT

Located at Purok 1, Brgy. Lugui, Labo, Camarines Norte



Isa sa pinakauna at dinarayong nature resort sa Cam.Norte!


Malinis at sobrang lamig ng tubig
Free flowing ang tubig na galing sa ilog ng Labo
Presko, maraming puno at nakakarelax pa
Entrance Fee:
Adult- Php 30
Kids-Php 20
Picnic Cottages - Php 400-600
Picnic Table- Php 200



How to get there?
Galing Labo Terminal, sakay ka ng tricycle na papuntang Brgy. Lugui
tapos sabihin lang kay kuyang drayber na ibaba ka sa Sinagtala Resort.
OPEN from 8 am til 5pm
*No Overnight Stay
IMPORTANT REMINDERS:
Be a responsible traveller. Always practice Leave No Trace (LNT) & Clean As You Go (Claygo) principles.
Respect the place, Strictly No Vandalism at Bawal po ang pasaway!
