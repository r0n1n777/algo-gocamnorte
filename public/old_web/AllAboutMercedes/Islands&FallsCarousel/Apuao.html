Apuao Island

Mercedes, Camarines Norte

Mercedes Group of Islands is one of the underrated islands in the Philippines but now becoming a favorite destination by backpackers and tourists because of its white sand beaches, pine trees and protected coral and fish sanctuaries.

Mercedes, is one of the towns of Camarines Norte which lies at the eastern coast of the province. The islands are just 1 hour boat ride away from the mainland.





How to Get to Mercedes


By Air

Take a flight from Manila to Pili, Camarines Sur then have a 15-minute jeep/bus ride to Naga City. From Naga City, take a van bound to Daet which is about 2 hours ride then ride a tricycle or jeep from Daet to Mercedes.


By Land

From Cubao, take a bus bound to Daet (Superlines, DLTB, Philtranco) which is about 8-10 hours ride from Manila. Fare ranges from P550- P650. When you arrive in Daet, take another 15-minute ride to Mercedes.



GOING TO THE ISLAND:
You can rent a boat for P3500-4500 depending on its size and capacity good for two-day and 1 night stay and island hopping. But if you want your tours to be arranged ahead of time, you may Contact Mercedes Tourism Office at (054) 444-1261.
