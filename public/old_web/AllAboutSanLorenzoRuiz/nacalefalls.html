Nacali Falls

Brgy. Maisog, San Lorenzo Ruiz, Luzon 4610 Philippines

Nacali Falls is one of the many falls in the Province of Camarines Norte.
San Lorenzo Ruiz can be reach by jeepney from Daet Integrated Terminal.
The water in the falls is cold and relaxing.
Tram line can be an optional side trip on the tour.




Reaching this cold and relaxing 60 ft. waterfalls will take you around 1 Â½ to 2 hours of hiking through vast pineapple plantation, coconut trees and dense forest. Tourist have an option to ride the longest zip line in Bicol or the Matacong-maisog tramline to lessen the hikeand at the same time, they will get to see enchanting views that is truly a memorable experience.
