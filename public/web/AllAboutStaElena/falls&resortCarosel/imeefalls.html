Imee Irene Twin Falls

Located at Sitio Lumok, Brgy. Bulala, Sta. Elena, Camarines Norte



It is a picturesque duo of falls named after the daughters of a former Philippine President. According
to an urban legend by a local townsfolk, then the President Ferdinand E. Marcos has ordered a
treasure hunting in the area and successfully unearthed gold bars that is why there was a concrete
pool created in front of the waterfall which signifies the spot where the excavation occurred. This
intriguing yet refreshing twin falls can be reached via a 5-10mins short hike from its jump-off point.
