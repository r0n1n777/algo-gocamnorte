Ansiong Falls

Located at Brgy. Mangkasay, Paracale, Camarines Norte


Dubbed as the “Falls ng mga Sawi”, It was name after Venancio T. Lauce, an intelligent English speaking gentlemen from mangkasay who choose to spent all his life in this thick forest as a result of being heartbroken to a girl named “Amor” who hails from nearby Brgy. Calaburnay.He eventually died on 1997 on a cave just within the area.
