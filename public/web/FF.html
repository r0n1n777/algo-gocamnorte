FUN FACTS

Rafflesia


The world’s largest flower can be found in Mt. Guinatungan, Barangay San Isidro, San Lorenzo Ruiz. Rafflesia (Rafflesiaceace) is a genus of parasitic plants growing in the forested lowlands and low to mid-elevation mountain forest of Thailand, Peninsular Malaysia, Sumatra, Java, Borneo and the Philippines

Bagasbas Beach


Bagasbas Beach is the first Internationally recognized surfing spot in the Philippines which attracts both local and foreign surfers. Currently, it is the 62nd top surfing spot in the world. It also has the largest number of accredited surfing instructions in Bicol Region thus in very ideal for surf beginners.

Gateway to CamNorte


Sta. Elena is the first town of Cam. Norte thus the gateway to Bicol. It is dubbed as the “Zigzag Capital” of the Philippines because of its long stretch of winding and scenic highway.

Provincial Capitol

The 12 massive pillars of Camarines Norte’s Provincial Capital Building represents the 12 municipalities of the province.
