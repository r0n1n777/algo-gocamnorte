Bantayong Monument
Bantayog, the Rizal monument is a masonic inspired monument that is said to be made of coral stones which were taken from old Spanish prison house where many Katepuneros were tortured and excuted. The Bantayog was declared a national historical landbark by the National historical Commision in 1961, Finally in 2008, its status was elevated into a national monument by virtue of Resolution No. 12 "Declaring The Dr. Jose Rizal Monumennt in the Municipalityof Daet, Camarines Norte a National MOnument".

St. Peter the Apostle Church
The St. Peter the Apostle Chruch , also known as the Parroquia de San Pedro Apostol, is a Roman Catholic chruch located in the municipality of Vinzons in Camarines Notre, Philippines. It is the Oldest Church in Camarines Norte built by thr Franciccan friars in 1611.

Bantayog Festival
CamNorte's largest festival is the Bantayog Festival held every April 15 to mark the provincial founding day. It marks the covergance of the local festival of the various municipalities through socio-civic activities, tourism oriented events, sporting tournaments and street dance competition.
