Selfie Beach

A modern and stylish beach resort and hotel catering for
private romantic days to large celebrations, adding style and
luxury to create wonderful days to remember.



Umbrella Cabanas

Our umbrella cabanas are available for small parties to wine and dine or simply
relax close to the Bar and Ballroom Restaurant. Situated right on the edge
of the beach with views of the ocean it is the perfect base to create a day to remember.


Selfie Hotel


Featured in a central position at Selfie Beach you will find the Selfie Hotel.
A two story building containing a row of 10 large modern furnished suites.

Hotel Suites

Hotel Suites Comfort and elegance are the guiding concept behind our 10 guest rooms. Soak up the luxurious
privacy of a suite, which features an elegant
air-conditioned spacious bedroom/lounge with flat screen tv, your own designer
wet room with luxurious amenities and private balcony.
